Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Beyond Bliss Creations ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect:

  • Contact details — name, email, phone number
  • Shipping address — full address, city, state, pincode
  • Payment information — handled securely by Razorpay; we do not store your card or UPI details on our servers
  • Order history — items purchased, dates, amounts
  • Communication — WhatsApp messages, emails, and call records related to your orders

How we use your information

  • Process and ship your orders
  • Communicate about your order status, delivery updates, and any issues
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and tracking information
  • Improve our products, service, and website
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, GST, accounting)

Sharing your information

We share your information only with:

  • Courier partners (Delhivery, DTDC, Bluedart, etc.) — to deliver your order
  • Payment processors (Razorpay) — to process payments securely
  • Service providers we use to run our business (hosting, email, analytics) — under strict confidentiality
  • Government authorities — only when legally required

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Marketing communication

We may occasionally send you updates on new products, sales, or festive offers via WhatsApp, email, or SMS. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP on WhatsApp or clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption on our website
  • Payments processed via PCI-compliant Razorpay infrastructure
  • Limited access to customer data within our team

However, no online transmission is 100% secure. While we do our best, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies & browser storage

Our website uses your browser's local storage to remember items in your shopping cart between visits. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising. We may use simple analytics to understand which pages visitors find useful — this data is anonymised.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Delete your data (subject to legal record-keeping requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time

To exercise any of these rights, WhatsApp us at +91 97975 92768.

Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version. Significant changes will be communicated via the website.
