Our Story

Saharanpur — the wood-carving capital of India.

This city produces over 70% of India's wooden handicrafts. For decades, our artisan partners have shaped sheesham, mango, and rosewood into pieces that families across India keep for years.

We bring this craft directly to you — without middlemen marking up 4× along the way. Honest pieces. Honest prices. Free shipping, always.
