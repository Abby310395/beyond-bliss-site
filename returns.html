Policy

Returns & Refunds

Last updated: April 2026

We want you to love every piece you receive from Beyond Bliss Creations. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

What can be returned

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for the following reasons:

  • Damaged in transit — broken, cracked, or chipped on arrival
  • Defective product — manufacturing defect, missing parts, or significant quality issue
  • Wrong item shipped — you received something different from what you ordered

What cannot be returned

  • Customised, engraved, or personalised orders
  • Bulk and corporate gifting orders (refer to your bulk order agreement)
  • Products damaged after delivery due to misuse or accidents
  • Items returned without original packaging
  • Products marked "Final Sale" or sold at clearance discount
Note on minor variations: Each piece is hand-carved from natural wood. Slight variations in grain, colour, knots, and finish are characteristics of authentic handicraft, not defects. These do not qualify for return.

How to request a return

  1. WhatsApp us at +91 97975 92768 within 7 days of delivery
  2. Share your order number, the issue, and clear photos of the product and packaging
  3. Our team responds within 24 hours with next steps
  4. If approved, we arrange a reverse pickup OR ask you to ship back to our Saharanpur address
  5. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund or replacement is processed

Refund timelines

Once your return is received and approved:

  • UPI / Razorpay payments — refund credited within 5–7 business days
  • Cards / Net Banking — refund credited within 7–10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Cash on Delivery — refund via UPI or bank transfer within 5–7 business days

Replacement

If you'd prefer a replacement instead of a refund, we'll ship a new piece once the original is returned. Replacement shipping is free.

Cancellations

You can cancel your order before it is dispatched for a full refund. Once dispatched, the order falls under our standard returns policy.

To cancel, WhatsApp us as soon as possible with your order number.

Important: Please record an unboxing video of higher-value orders (above ₹1,500). This helps us process damage claims faster with our courier partners.
