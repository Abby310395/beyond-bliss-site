Policy

Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: April 2026

At Beyond Bliss Creations, we ship every order from Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh — the wood-carving capital of India — directly to your door, anywhere in India.

Free shipping, always

We offer FREE shipping pan-India on all orders. There are no minimum order values, hidden charges, or surprise fees at checkout. The price you see is the price you pay.

Delivery timelines

RegionEstimated delivery
Lucknow & nearby UP cities2–4 business days
Delhi NCR, North India3–5 business days
Mumbai, Pune, Western India4–6 business days
Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad & South India5–7 business days
Northeast, J&K, Andaman & remote PIN codes7–10 business days

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp and email once your order is shipped.

Packaging

Wooden handicrafts are delicate. Every piece is:

  • Wrapped in protective bubble wrap or tissue
  • Cushioned with paper or foam padding
  • Packed in a sturdy corrugated box
  • Sealed and labelled with care

Order tracking

Once dispatched, you will receive:

  • An order confirmation message on WhatsApp
  • The courier name (Delhivery, DTDC, Bluedart, India Post or similar)
  • A tracking ID and link to follow your shipment

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery on most pin codes. A small COD handling fee may apply at checkout. To check COD availability for your area, simply enter your pincode at checkout — the system will confirm.

Damaged or missing items

If your package arrives damaged, please:

  1. Don't accept the package if visible damage is severe — ask the courier to mark it "refused due to damage"
  2. If accepted, take clear photos of the package and the damaged item within 24 hours of delivery
  3. WhatsApp us at +91 97975 92768 with the photos and your order number

We'll arrange a replacement or refund — see our Returns & Refunds policy for details.

Delivery delays

Occasionally, delays beyond our control may occur due to:

  • Courier service disruptions
  • Weather, festivals, or local lockdowns
  • Incorrect or incomplete address
  • Recipient unavailable for delivery

We'll keep you informed via WhatsApp if any delays are expected.

Tip: Please double-check your shipping address and active phone number at checkout. We are unable to redirect parcels once dispatched.
